
WARNING
-------

Do NOT proceed unless you have:

(i) a version TWO (2) PiEconetBridge HAT installed and
(ii) it has had its EEPROM write line enabled with a solder bridge
(iii) included the following two lines in your config.txt:

dtparam=i2c_arm=on
dtparam=i2c_vc=on

(iv) rebooted before running making this target.

You can contact @KenLowe on the stardot forums for information on
how to enable the write line.

Press RETURN to continue, or Ctrl-C to exit...
